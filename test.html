Story Line

In the beginning, mysterious manuscripts appear, unveiling secrets hidden from humanity for centuries...

Each page reveals dark truths about history, religion, and power — a puzzle leading to the unknown.

The characters are trapped in a journey of fear and discovery, where nothing is as it seems...

Until the final revelation comes — terrifying and shocking, changing the way one sees the world forever.

The introduction is a warning — a dark gateway that tells the reader they are about to uncover hidden truths and forbidden knowledge. Once entered, there is no way back. The story explores Nimrod, the mighty king who defied God, built towers, and sought total control over humanity, symbolizing extreme ambition. Lucifer, the fallen angel, embodies rebellion and pride, choosing absolute freedom even at the cost of evil, sparking the eternal struggle between good and evil.
