About the Novel

Antichristos is not just a novel. It is a doorway into the hidden corners of history, the secrets time tried to bury, and truths too dark for the light.

Written by Egyptian author DR Ahmed Khaled Mostafa, the novel threads together ancient conspiracies, forbidden knowledge, and the quiet war between good and evil. Each chapter peels back a layer: secret societies, coded histories, and unseen hands shaping the fate of mankind.

This is a tale where darkness whispers louder than silence—where every clue leads deeper into the unknown, and every answer demands a heavier question.
